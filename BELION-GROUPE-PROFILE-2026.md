## About Us

Belion Groupe Limited is a diversified technology and multimedia company established in **2019**, delivering innovative B2B and B2C solutions across **AgriTech, EduTech, FinTech, Mining, PropTech, E-commerce**, and other emerging technology sectors.

Driven by a vision to enhance the affordability of abundant life through technology, the company provides smart, scalable, and customer-focused digital solutions supported by strong after-sales service and technical support.

Belion Groupe Limited is committed to creating value through innovation, empowering businesses and communities, and becoming a leading provider of **Technology Services, Internet Value Added Services, and Multimedia Solutions**.
